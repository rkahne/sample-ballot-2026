About the May 19, 2026 Primary Election

Democratic Primary Races

Only registered Democrats vote in these races. Only contested races (2+ candidates) appear on the primary ballot — uncontested candidates go directly to the November general election.

Nonpartisan Races (Mayor & Metro Council)

All voters participate regardless of party. These races are nonpartisan — party affiliation will NOT appear on your official ballot. We've included registration info here so you know who you're voting for. Only races with 3+ candidates have a primary.
